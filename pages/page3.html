Investment World

I've read a statement about the main difference between rich and poor, winners and losers. Which is the way they treat the loss.
"The loss inspires the winners to become better and scares the fuck out of losers".
The same principle works with the money world. Smart but afraid to lose money people never become wealthy, they usually invest money in stable companies and their portforlio consists of stocks with minimal risks. Thats undoubtedly already a smart move but they have just a little chance of becoming rich. On the other hand wealthy people invest their money into small but perspective start-ups. And its always more risky to do. However they somehow make a fortunes thankfully to this plan. I was reading rich dad poor dad where the author said that he has never seen a rich person that havent ever lost a single dollar. So maybe thats where all the answers to my questions lie.
